About Us

About Elementary Edge

Our mission, in our own words

Elementary Edge is dedicated to making learning simple, effective, and accessible for students from Nursery to Class 12. We provide both home tuition and online classes, designed to give personalized attention to every student.

Our tutors are college students, graduates, and postgraduate learners who bring 2–4 years of teaching experience. Being close to the latest syllabus and exam patterns, they understand student needs better and explain concepts in a simple and relatable way.

We offer subject-focused guidance in English, Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, helping students improve their understanding, confidence, and academic performance.

At Elementary Edge, we believe the right guidance should come to you. Whether at home or online, we ensure a comfortable and supportive learning environment for every child.
